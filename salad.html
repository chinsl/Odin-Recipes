Salad

Description

This is the salad recipe. It is important to include greens in your diet, and salads are a popular means to do so. This salad is a simple one!

Ingredients

  • spring greens
  • coconut aminos
  • apple cider vinegar
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Add two cups of spring greens
  2. Add a cap-full of apple cider vinegar
  3. Add two tablespoons of olive oil
  4. Toss salad
  5. Add coconut aminos to taste
