Smoothie

Description

This is the smoothie recipe. Smoothies are an efficient and tasty way to quickly supply your body with essential micronutrients. This recipe primarily uses berries, but you can add anything you want!

Ingredients

  • one cup of mixed berries
  • four tablespoons ofpeanut butter
  • half a cup of pineapple chunks
  • protein powder
  • two cups of water

Steps

  1. Add mixed berries
  2. Add peanut butter
  3. Add pineapple chunks
  4. Add water
  5. Blend
