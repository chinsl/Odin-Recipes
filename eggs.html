Eggs

Description

This is the recipe for boiled eggs. Eggs make for a nutritious, satisfying breakfast, lunch, or dinner. This recipe details the secrets to perfectly boiling eggs.

Ingredients

Steps

  • eggs
  • water
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Place eggs into a 2qt saucepan
  2. Pour water to one inch above eggs
  3. Bring to boil, then cover for 10 minutes
  4. Drain and cool in ice bath
  5. Peel!
